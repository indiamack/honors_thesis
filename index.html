UNCOVERING

A SHIPWRECK

in the Pamlico Sound

by India Mackinson

The Pappy’s Lane shipwreck was forgotten. Its story eroded away over the decades as oysters settled on it disappearing frame, leaving behind vague recollections from local residents and no written record. But once put in the path of a major infrastructure project, this insignificant, unknown wreck revealed an identity at the center of an even bigger story: man versus nature. Read on to explore the intersections between science, history, environment and people on Hatteras Island, a resilient and dynamic community that’s survived centuries of wind and water.
